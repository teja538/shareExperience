Placements

On-campus: These campus placements are arranged by your college who invite all the best possible companies to recruit from their students.

Off-campus: Off-campus placement is when you get placed at a company without your college being involved in the process. In case of off-campus placements, you need to directly send job applications to the companies that you wish to join.

On-campus

Product based companies: The product-based companies are those companies which deal with a tangible product. A tangible product is a product which we can see or touch.
eg: Apple provides the cell phones iPad iPod MacBook
Google provides Gmail Google Drive Google map, etc.

Service based companies: The service offered by the company is intangible and can be provided in the form of skills, amenities, and expertise It gives software solutions to other companies.
eg: Tata Consultancy Services, Wipro, Cognizant, Infosys etc.
**search on YouTube or google to get cleared about the difference between them
